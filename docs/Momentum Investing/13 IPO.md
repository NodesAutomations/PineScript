### Different types

- retails- individual investor with shares less than 2 lakh rupee
- QIB- Qualified institutional buyers- mutual funds, NBFC, investement management companies
- NII- Non institutional Investors
    - Small HNI- individual applicants who apply for shares worth 2 lakh to 10
    - Big HNI-10lakh or more
- Allotment happens on lottery basis
